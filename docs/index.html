Koine Per Se Illustrata

A graded reader for New Testament Greek

KPSI is an inductive graded reader for Koine Greek, designed to bring the learner from absolute beginner to fluent reading of the Greek New Testament. Following the method of LLPSI and LGPSI, vocabulary and grammar are introduced gradually through continuous narrative, never through explicit translation or rule tables.

The story is set in Corinth in the 50s AD — the world of Paul's letters and the Acts of the Apostles. The narrative introduces all core NT vocabulary and grammar, transitioning in the final chapters to unadapted New Testament texts.
