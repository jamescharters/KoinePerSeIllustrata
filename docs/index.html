Ἡ Κοινὴ καθ᾿ αὑτὴν φωτιζομένη

Koine Per Se Illustrata

A graded reader for New Testament Greek

KPSI is an inductive graded reader for Koine Greek, designed to bring the learner from alphabet to fluent reading of the Greek New Testament. Following the method of LLPSI and LGPSI, vocabulary and grammar are introduced gradually through continuous original narrative — never through explicit translation or rule tables.

The story is set in Corinth in 51 AD, following Χρύσιππος, a Greek freedman merchant, as his world is drawn into the community forming around Aquila, Priscilla, Crispus, and Paul — the historical figures named in Acts 18 and 1 Corinthians. Authentic NT texts appear in every chapter from the first, growing in length and complexity as the reader's Greek grows with them.
